about

Our vision is to be the builder of choice for value minded clients and high performing employees, mission is developing high performing people that deliver successful projects.

Founded in 1995 İlk Construction is a full-service construction firm offering design build, preconstruction, construction management and general contracting services to meet our clients’ every need. We are dedicated to maintaining commitments to each and every one of our valued customers.

At İlk Construction, we protect our reputation for excellent customer service while providing the highest quality with competitive pricing. We make this possible through our absolute insistence upon utilizing responsible subcontractors qualified for the specific project and maintaining open dialogue with our past, current and potential clients.

SERVICES

With every project, we implement a series of procedures that provides critical guidelines by which each component of the project may be carefully and precisely monitored. We actively participate with our clients and the design team during each stage of the project to ensure a project that produces the best value within the budget.

Construction

İlk Construction has a broad range of experience working with both public and private organizations. Construction is managed by the designated project team who are responsible to maintain the established budget and schedule while maintaining tight control on the quality of the work and a safe work environment. We emphasize an open and direct communication with our clients and the design team during the construction period to be sure all parties are aware of any impacts to the budget, schedule or quality of work.

roofing

İlk COnstruction has offered roofing expertise for commercial and industrial property managers and owners since 1997. We are highly experienced in installing, maintaining and repairing roofing systems for multifamily apartment complexes, industrial buildings, retail shopping centers and other commercial buildings. Whether your metal roof or gutters need repair, or you’re ready to install a brand new roof, you can trust our experienced team of roofers to do the job right the first time and always offer the best customer service.

restoration

Whether you’re looking to preserve, modernize or restore your historic facility, our team brings expertise in working with local regulations and standards required to handle structural preservation requirements and historic protection designations. We understand the complexities and uniqueness associated with your historic building restoration project, from a government building to a museum or theater and we draw from our extensive experience and state-of-the-art tools to ensure quality and the timely delivery of your restoration or renovation project.

design

Cost, time, buildability, durability, function and aesthetics are key considerations in the design of buildings and must be carefully balanced to meet the needs of the owner and end user without compromising on safety and sustainability. Listening to client needs and creating integrated designs that are effective and affordable are important drivers for our teams. We pride ourselves on being skilled at integrating people, space and technology.
